Chromatik

Welcome to our p5.js project! This project is designed to showcase the capabilities of the p5.js library, a powerful tool for creating interactive visualizations and animations.

Overview

This project is a web-based interactive visualization that allows users to explore the concept of music theory through color theory. By selecting different intervals and mixing them together, users can experiment with the buidling blocks of music and gain a deeper understanding of how intervals work.
